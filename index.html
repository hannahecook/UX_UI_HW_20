United Angels









About the United Angels Foundation

The United Angels Foundation (UAF) is a non-profit, parent-to-parent support group. The primary mission of the foundation is to support parents and families of children with special needs. All donations are 100% tax deductible, and 100% of the donations contributed are used to benefit the members of UAF through support group activities and functions, educational materials, hospital packages, and much much more. The Board of Directors for UAF are not compensated whatsoever; which means that contributors can feel confident that every dollar they provide to United Angels will be used to support these wonderful children, and their families.



Problem

Solution

The United Angels Foundation website does not clearly state the purpose of the foundation or the call to action to donate.
By simplifying and unifying all the webpages containing elements to donate into one central call-to-action space we can give visitors clarity into who UAF is, how to donate, and transparency into how donations are being used.



Research


We conducted research by first creating a research plan and then interviewing multiple users about why they donate or volunteer with nonprofits. We also created a survey that garnered over 10 responses to similar questions. These questions consisted of what volunteers look for in a non-profit, what is most important to them, and things pertaining specifically to children with special needs.  Some key findings that we had was that most volunteers care most about the mission of an organization, leadership & organization, and transparency.  After researching this issue, we found that competitors have transparency in their mission and ease of a donation process. United Angels Foundation was text-heavy making it hard to understand their purpose. The donation button was also hidden in the navigation bar and did not stand out.
